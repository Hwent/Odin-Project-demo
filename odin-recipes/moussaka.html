Moussaka

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced lengthwise into 1/4-inch thick slices
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Brush both sides of eggplant slices with olive oil, and place them on a baking sheet. Bake until lightly browned, turning once.
  3. Heat lamb in a skillet over medium heat. Cook until evenly browned. Stir in onion and garlic, and cook until onion is tender. Drain off excess fat. Season with cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice, ginger, black pepper, salt, parsley and tomato paste. Stir in tomato sauce and wine, and simmer for 20 minutes. Remove from heat, and set aside.
  4. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in flour until smooth. Cook, stirring until light brown. Gradually whisk in milk, then add nutmeg and salt. Cook, stirring constantly, until thickened. Remove from heat, and stir in 1/4 cup of the Parmesan cheese and the eggs.
  5. Place a layer of eggplant in the bottom of a greased 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover eggplant with all of the meat mixture, and then sprinkle 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese over the meat. Cover with remaining eggplant, and sprinkle another 1/4 cup of cheese on top. Pour the milk mixture over the top, and sprinkle with nutmeg and remaining cheese.
  6. Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
